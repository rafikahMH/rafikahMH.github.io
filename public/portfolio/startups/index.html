Engaging with Singapore's start-up scene with NUS Entrepreneurship Society

Aug 2016

Being involved as the President of the NUS Entrepreneurship Society in 2016, I was very much engaged within the start-up scene in Singapore, and still is.

The highlights of the year included:

Inaugural Internship Fair


We successfully liaised with over 20 corporate partners such as Uber, e27 and many other start-ups to hold the inaugural internship fair. The event saw over 300 budding entrepreneurs and start-up enthusiasts from all over Singapore (top three universities: NUS, NTU and SMU) who came down to NUS campus to seek internship opportunities from exciting start-ups. Truly an amazing networking haven!

Nation-wide Student Engagement


As part of the student engagement pipeline, we called down many founders from start-ups all over Singapore such as TribeHired, Happy Marketer, and VersaFleet to speak and share with aspiring student entrepreneurs. Events included: industry awareness series, workshops and bootcamps.

Entrepreneurial Blog


As part of digital engagement, we created a website and blog for entrepreneurial tips and events. You can visit it here.
